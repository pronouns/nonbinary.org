'''JD Samson''' is a musician with the bands Le Tigre and MEN. She identifies as [[genderqueer]] and a gender outlaw.

She said of her involvement with Le Tigre: “I was this genderqueer performer and artist that kind of helped push along a new … revolution in identity”.&lt;ref&gt;[http://nyunews.com/2013/12/10/samson/ "Musician JD Samson discusses career, creation of MEN"], Washington Square News, Dec 2013&lt;/ref&gt;

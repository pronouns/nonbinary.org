[[Fluid Gender]] aka '''Genderfluid''', '''Pangender''', '''Polygender''' is an identity under the [[nonbinary]], [[genderqueer]] and [[transgender]] umbrellas. Fluid gender individuals move between two or more different gender identities or expressions at different times or in different situations.

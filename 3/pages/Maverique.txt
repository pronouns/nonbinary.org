[http://maveriques.tumblr.com/faq http://maveriques.tumblr.com/faq]&lt;/ref&gt; is a specific [[nonbinary]] [[gender identity]] "characterized by autonomy and inner conviction regarding a sense of self that is entirely independent of male/masculinity, female/femininity or anything which derives from the two while still being neither without gender nor of a neutral gender."&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 26, 2014. It also isn't a lack of gender, or an apathetic attitude about gender.&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. "Maverique." ''Maveriques.'' [http://maveriques.tumblr.com/maverique http://maveriques.tumblr.com/maverique]&lt;/ref&gt; Maverique is strictly a gender identity, not a [[gender expression]], so "how a maverique person chooses to express or present themself is entirely up to that individual."&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 16, 2014. ==History==
In 2014, Vesper H. (queerascat) created the word "maverique" to describe their own gender.&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 1, 2014. [http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/87401571441/this-is-me-attempting-to-further-define-my-own http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/87401571441/this-is-me-attempting-to-further-define-my-own]&lt;/ref&gt;  The word comes from "maverick" and the French suffix "-ique,"&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 26, 2014. [http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/89448452041/maverique-definition-reworded-06-21-14-a http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/89448452041/maverique-definition-reworded-06-21-14-a]&lt;/ref&gt; and can be used as a noun ("some maveriques") or an adjective ("some maverique people").&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 16, 2014. [http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/88914739161/hi-i-love-the-new-term-you-coined-wrt-gender http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/88914739161/hi-i-love-the-new-term-you-coined-wrt-gender]&lt;/ref&gt;

==Difference from other genders==
Maverique is similar to, but not the same as, other nonbinary identities such as [[aporagender]]&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 15, 2014. [http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/88814996996/so-i-just-stumbled-upon-a-new-gender-that-has http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/88814996996/so-i-just-stumbled-upon-a-new-gender-that-has]&lt;/ref&gt;, [[neutrois]]&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 16, 2014. [http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/88861493366/maverique-why-i-coined-the-word http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/88861493366/maverique-why-i-coined-the-word]&lt;/ref&gt;, or [[gendermaverick]].&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 19, 2014. [http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/89161940391/gendermaverick-a-definition http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/89161940391/gendermaverick-a-definition]&lt;/ref&gt; Maverique is not a lack of a gender identity, so it isn't the same as [[agender]] or genderless. Unlike [[nonbinary identities worldwide|ethnic nonbinary gender identities]] such as [[Two-Spirit]] and [[third gender]], maverique is not an identity exclusive to any ethnic group.&lt;ref&gt;Vesper H. (queerascat). June 17, 2014. [http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/89056304496/is-it-okay-to-use-maverique-if-i-am-white-i-just http://queerascat.tumblr.com/post/89056304496/is-it-okay-to-use-maverique-if-i-am-white-i-just]&lt;/ref&gt;  Anyone of any ethnic background can take up the word maverique for themselves, if they feel that it describes them.&lt;ref&gt;"FAQ." ''Maveriques.'' [http://maveriques.tumblr.com/faq http://maveriques.tumblr.com/faq]&lt;/ref&gt; It's never [[ethnicity and culture#Cultural appropriation|cultural appropriation]].

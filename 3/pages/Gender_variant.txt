[[Gender variant]] is an [[umbrella term]] for [[gender identity|gender identities]] and [[gender expression|expressions]] that are different than the norm. That is, they are to some degree [[transgender]], and don't fit into the [[gender binary]] system. Gender variant has variously been used as another word for [[genderqueer]], [[nonbinary]], transgender, [[third gender]], and [[gender nonconforming]].

==History==

"Gender variant" was among the 56 genders made available on Facebook in 2014.&lt;ref&gt;Eve Shapiro, ''Gender circuits: Bodies and identities in a technological age.'' Unpaged.&lt;/ref&gt;
